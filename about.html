About ImageActionBot

ImageActionBot is automation for Windows and Android that anyone can approach like a normal app: you work from pictures of your screen (buttons, fields, icons) and simple step choices — not from code. Underneath, the software handles image matching, typing, key actions, and structured runs so repetitive work becomes repeatable and calmer.

What we focus on

  • Ease of understanding: If you can point at what should happen on screen, you can turn it into a step.
  • No mandatory programming: Menus and a clear step list instead of scripts for everyday automation.
  • Serious execution: Multi-step flows, data from spreadsheets, document layouts, and controls like Run All, Test, and emergency stop when a run is active.

Why this matters

People and teams lose hours to the same clicks and typing every day. ImageActionBot exists to compress that work into flows you trust — saved, documented with our guides, and runnable on your own PC.

ImageActionBot is developed and maintained by ImageActionBot Technologies, an independent software initiative focused on building practical automation tools for everyday computer users.

The goal is simple: less repetitive manual work, more time for work that actually needs a human — without asking users to become developers first.

The software is designed privacy-first: your workflows and screen templates run on your computer; we do not rely on cloud processing for core automation.

ImageActionBot keeps improving from real usage and feedback — the same bar we use for tools we would run on our own machines.
